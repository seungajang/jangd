Beint Messenger / 2018
An instant chat solution for enterprise
Team chat’s main benefit is that it keeps all your company’s communications in one place, making it easier for everyone to talk together with group or private chats.
Currently Beint messenger services are provided in-house use
Overview
Beint helps organize team conversations about multiple topics, search through your company archives to see if a question has already been answered, and speed up with bots and integrations that bring apps into your chats.
Role
UX/UI Designer of IT Development / MOTREX.
Motrex Co., Ltd operates in the car infotainment and connected technology development field in South Korea and internationally. The company offers in vehicle infotainment products, such as audio visual navigation and video, around view monitor, head up display, and rear seat entertainment products.
My role during the course of this project was providing design direction and build up every single design of the product, including publishing. I’ve been improving performance and usability for the messenger.
Problem space
It all started out with the following observation.
1. Too many ways to talk to our team (by emails, traditional phone calls and social networks. Even SMS could work)
2. Detecting a fault in the system that we used
Insight
1. No more searching for a team member’s email address or their phone numbers
2. Keeping all your company’s communications in one place
3. Starting a new chat, adding the people you need, and start talking about it as easy as possible
4. Making it easier for everyone to talk together with group or private chats
5. Integration with Other services such as a to-do app, groupware, email and ERP
Concept direction
1. One on one talk, group chat, broadcasting
2. Messaging of texts, video chats, screen sharing and files
3. Reflecting organizational changes in real time
